  • Should I apply for an F visa or an L visa?
    Both are good. Although F looks more relevant, if you are a U.S. citizen, the advantage for an L visa would be the eligibility for 10-year multiple entry. See NOTE 1 here for details.

  • Travel adapter
    The voltage in China is 220V at 50Hz, if your device's charger is 2-pin plug, you can handle it fine without a transformer.
    But if it's 3-pins, you should take a travel adapter with you. Please refer here for the Chinese sockets.
  • Laundry facilities
    The Guest House has laundry facilities, including dryers, free of charge. They are located in the bathrooms on the 3rd floor of Block 2 (the building where you check in).
  • Wi-Fi, eduroam, VPN
    Guest House: Free wifi with password is available.
    Elsewhere on campus: SUSTech is a participating institution of eduroam.
    Shenzhen airport：Free wifi "AirportSZ" and "SZairport", log in with your boarding information.
    Hongkong airport: Free wifi is available. Floor sockets are mostly available in the waiting area inside of security check; in the outer non-restricted area, there are charging booths for electronic devices, but floor sockets are only available in certain restaurants, such as Pokka Café.

    VPN: You will need a VPN from your home institution or by purchase in order to access certain websites while in China, including Gmail, YouTube and certain social media sites.

  • Shenzhen Bay Park
    Extends along the seashore and connects the last two clusters of restaurants above. Good for cycling, running, walking, or people-watching.
  • OCT LOFT
    The Overseas Chinese Town (OCT, not OCT East) is located in the heart of Nanshan District's living area (SUSTech is at the north end), surrounded by trees and green space. The LOFT is a popular spot for young people, with (contemporary) art exhibits, book and antique stores, cafes, and is connected to other parts of the OCT within walking distance, including the Happy Coast above. (See also the first Shenzhen video at the bottom of this page.)
